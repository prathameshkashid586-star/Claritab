Claritab Privacy Policy

Claritab does not collect, store or transmit any personal data.

Tab titles and URLs are read locally to display, search and group your tabs. This data never leaves your device.

Page content is extracted only when you explicitly request an AI summary or research comparison. This content is sent directly to your chosen AI provider (Anthropic, Google, OpenAI or Groq) using your own API key.

API keys are stored locally on your device only and are never sent to any server other than the AI provider you choose.

No user data is shared with third parties.
